  1. Kung Pao Chicken

    2. Yields: 4 servings

    Prep time: 20 minutes

    Cook time: 15 minutes

    ingredients

    • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
    • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
    • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
    • 1/2 teaspoon sesame oil
    • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
    • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
    • 1/2 cup peanuts
    • 3 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 inch ginger, grated
    • 1/4 cup dry sherry
    • 2 tablespoons honey
    • 1 tablespoon chili oil
    • 1 teaspoon Sichuan peppercorns
    • 1/4 teaspoon salt

    Instruction

    1. Marinate the chicken: In a small bowl, combine the cornstarch, soy sauce, and sesame oil. Add the chicken pieces and toss to coat. Let marinate for 15 minutes.
    2. Prepare the sauce: In a small bowl, combine the dry sherry, honey, chili oil, Sichuan peppercorns, and salt.
    3. Cook the chicken: Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over high heat. Add the marinated chicken and cook for 2-3 minutes, or until browned. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
    4. Sauté the vegetables: Add the red bell pepper, garlic, and ginger to the skillet. Cook for 1-2 minutes, or until fragrant.
    5. Add the sauce: Pour the sauce over the vegetables and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 1-2 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened.
    6. Return the chicken: Add the cooked chicken back to the skillet and toss to coat in the sauce. Cook for 1-2 minutes more, or until heated through.
    7. Add the peanuts: Stir in the peanuts and cook for 30 seconds, or until heated through.
    8. Serve: Serve immediately over rice.

  2. Chow Mein

    3. Yields: 4 servings

    Prep time: 20 minutes

    Cook time: 15 minutes

    ingredients

    • 1 pound dried egg noodles
    • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
    • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
    • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
    • 1 onion, sliced
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 inch ginger, grated
    • 1/4 cup soy sauce
    • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
    • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
    • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
    • 1/4 cup water

    Instruction

    1. Cook the noodles: Cook the noodles according to package directions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
    2. Heat the oil: Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over high heat.
    3. Cook the chicken: Add the chicken to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes, or until browned. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
    4. Sauté the vegetables: Add the red bell pepper, onion, garlic, and ginger to the skillet. Cook for 2-3 minutes, or until softened.
    5. Add the sauces: Stir in the soy sauce, oyster sauce, hoisin sauce, and sesame oil. Cook for 1 minute, or until the sauces are combined.
    6. Add the water: Add the water and bring to a simmer.
    7. Return the chicken: Add the cooked chicken and noodles to the skillet. Toss to coat in the sauce.
    8. Cook until heated through: Cook for 2-3 minutes, or until the noodles are heated through and the flavors have combined.
    9. Serve: Serve immediately.
